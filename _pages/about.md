## Biographies

Wang Zongmin was born in Anhui Province, China in 2001. During the years 2019 to 2023, he was pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering degree in Software Engineering at Northeastern University. Currently, he is pursuing a Master of Engineering degree in Software Engineering at Northeastern University. <br>  Currently, he is seeking a PhD position for his future academic career. His **research interests** includes: Cryptography, Trusted Computing, Access Control, Deep Learning Security, Information security, Malware Detection, Federated Learning.
